This application is a part of RS School Angular course.

RS School is free-of-charge and community-based education program conducted by The Rolling Scopes developer community since 2013.

The mentors and trainers of the school are front-end and javascript developers from different companies and countries.

Everyone can study at RS School, regardless of age, professional employment, or place of residence. However, you should have sufficient base knowledge before the program begins.
